## Research Focus

I develop data-driven tools for water systems by integrating SCADA and sensor data with hydraulic modeling. My work focuses on **leak detection**, **anomaly detection**, **system diagnostics**, and **operational decision-making** — enhancing the reliability of water distribution and stormwater systems.

Currently a Ph.D. candidate in the [Future Water Systems Lab](http://future-water.org/people/) at UT Austin (advisor: Prof. Matthew Bartos).
